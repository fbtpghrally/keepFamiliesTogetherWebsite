Keep Families Together

The Pittsburgh, rally
for Families Belong Together
happening June 30th in Mellon Square.

  • Learn More

The Pittsburgh rally for Families Belong Together, is an action to show support for immigrant families and a humane immigration policy.

We believe that the current treatment of immigrant families seeking asylum is cruel and inhumane, and we demand that our elected leaders make changes that respect the rights of individuals and families.

We believe that all people, regardless of national origin, deserve respect and kindness. We believe that the United States stands for justice. We believe that laws should protect and not harm. We believe that our elected leaders should represent the beliefs and will of the people they represent. We believe that public action is required to make our voices heard on behalf of those who cannot speak.

We encourage Pittsburghers to join us on June 30 to make clear that the current treatment of immigrant families is unacceptable. We plan for a peaceful, family-friendly event that brings together the community in support of a humane approach to immigration.
